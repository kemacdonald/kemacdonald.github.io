I am a Ph.D student in the Department of Psychology at Stanford University where I work with Michael Frank in the Language and Cognition Lab. My research brings together ideas from social learning theories, active learning, and goal-based vision to better understand how social contexts shape children's language acquisition.

Here are some representative papers:

  • MacDonald, K., LaMarr, T., Corina, D., Marchman V.A., & Fernald, A. (accepted). Real-time lexical comprehension in young children learning American Sign Language. Developmental Science [PDF] [paper site] [code repository]

  • MacDonald, K., Blonder, A., Marchman V.A., Fernald, A., & Frank, M.C. (2017). An information-seeking account of eye movements during spoken and signed language comprehension. Proceedings of the 39th Annual Meeting of the Cognitive Science Society. [PDF] [code repository]

  • MacDonald, K., Yurovsky, D., & Frank, M.C. (2017). Social cues modulate the representations underlying cross-situational learning. Cognitive Psychology. 94, 67–84. [PDF] [PDF preprint] [code repository] [paper site]

  • MacDonald, K., Schug, M., Chase, E. & Barth, H. (2013). My people, right or wrong? Minimal group membership disrupts children’s selective trust in testimony. Cognitive Development, 28, 247-259. [PDF]

I am also passionate about open and reproducible science. All of my experiments, data, and analysis code are openly available on my github page. And I have contributed to several projects to help make open science easier:

  • cogsci2016: an R-package that allows you to write a reproducible submission to the Annual Cognitive Science Society Conference. [R package]

  • COD: Data availability, reusability, and analytic reproducibility: Evaluating the impact of a mandatory open data policy at the journal Cognition. [PDF preprint] [osf page] [code repository]

  • childes-db: a flexible and reproducible interface to the Child Language Data Exchange System (CHILDES). [website]

  • childesr: a R package interface to the childes-db system. [R package API]
